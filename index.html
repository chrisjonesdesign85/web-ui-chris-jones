Are you doing research for a paper and need a way to keep track of all the papers, books, articles, etc. that you are using? Or maybe you just need a way to keep track of your ‘to-read’ list with all of the blog posts, videos, and social media links that you’ve been meaning to read or watch?

- Enter, Research Pal! This app is a place where all articles, blog posts, and other media items can be stored, categorized and displayed in a clean and easy-to-use interface.

- Our goal to help you organize your life. Take that, Marie Kondo!

Research



- The app will let you enter a title, category, description and link for each item that you want to keep track of. You can then display these as a card set in an easy-to-scan format. You can select a single category, or view everything in either chronological or alphabetical order. You can also do a text search to look for specific words or phrases. When you are done with an item you can mark complete. Finally, a login screen and authentication will keep your data secure from other users.


- A future release will add social interaction, with the ability to recommend items to other users and to view the cards of users that you have ‘friended’.
