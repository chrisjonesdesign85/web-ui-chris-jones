Proposal



Are you doing research for a paper and need a way to keep track of all the papers, books, articles, etc. that you are using? Or maybe you just need a way to keep track of your ‘to-read’ list with all of the blog posts, videos, and social media links that you’ve been meaning to read or watch?

- Enter, Research Pal! This app is a place where all articles, blog posts, and other media items can be stored, categorized and displayed in a clean and easy-to-use interface.

- Our goal to help you organize your life. Take that, Marie Kondo!
